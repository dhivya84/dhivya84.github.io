01 — Contextual AI Outreach

Engineering Authenticity through Prompt Engineering & News Synthesis

Overview 02 Analytics →
The Challenge

The "Generic" Trap

Standard networking automation often feels robotic and insincere, leading to low response rates and damaged professional reputations. The challenge was to build a system that synthesizes three distinct data layers before drafting a single word:

  • Historical Context: Past interactions, shared projects, and last meeting notes.
  • Live Triggers: Real-time news updates (e.g., company funding, product launches).
  • Tone Mirroring: Adapting the outreach style to match the user's natural professional voice.
Implementation

Prompt Engineering & GPT-4 Synthesis

I engineered a multi-stage prompt pipeline that utilizes GPT-4 as a reasoning engine rather than just a text generator.

Context Injection

The system retrieves the last 3 emails/messages with a contact to determine the "strength" and "formality" of the relationship.

Trigger Extraction

A Python-based scraping layer extracts the "key event" from a news snippet, ensuring the outreach focuses on a specific, relevant achievement.

Technical Breakthrough: By using few-shot prompting with examples of the user's *actual* successful outreach, the system achieved a 90% authenticity score in blind user testing.
Technical Depth

Handling Hallucinations

To prevent the AI from "inventing" shared history, I implemented a strict RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) constraint. The model is penalized if it generates specific details not present in the provided JSON context of past interactions.
