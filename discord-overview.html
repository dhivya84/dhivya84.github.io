Project Overview

Why This Project Matters

Discord has a critical participation gap: the vast majority of users who join a new community never post a single message. Without early engagement, communities stagnate, social value erodes, and users churn before realizing the benefits of belonging. This case study explores the problem from first principles — through user interviews, quantitative data, and competitive analysis — and proposes a product strategy, PRD, and prototype to close that gap.
