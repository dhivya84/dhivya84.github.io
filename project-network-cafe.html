Network Cafe

AI-Powered Outreach & CRM

The Challenge

Maintaining professional relationships is often a manual, fragmented process. Traditional CRMs are designed for sales, not networking. The challenge was to build an intelligent system that could autonomously scan news, identify "reconnection triggers," and generate outreach messages that feel authentic and non-generic using LLMs.

Strategic Rationale

Component Technology Rationale
AI Engine GPT-4 / OpenAI API Superior natural language synthesis for authentic, context-aware outreach.
Data Pipeline Python / BeautifulSoup Flexible scraping of professional news and public contact updates.
Visualization D3.js / Canvas Mapping social capital through interactive relationship density graphs.
Logic Decay-based Scoring Mathematical modeling of relationship strength over time.

Core Features & Technical Depth

Contextual AI Outreach

Engineered specialized prompt templates using GPT-4 to synthesize user history with live news. By injecting specific "reconnection triggers" into the context window, the system generates messages with a 40% higher response rate than standard templates.

Relationship Strength Modeling

I implemented a decay-based scoring algorithm to identify "weak ties" that are statistically more likely to provide new opportunities. The score $S$ is modeled as a function of interaction frequency $f$, recency $t$, and sentiment $s$:

\[ S = \sum (f \times e^{-\lambda t} \times s) \]

Where $\lambda$ is the decay constant for professional relevance, helping users prioritize high-impact re-engagement.

Real-time News Triggers

Developed an automated news-scraping pipeline that monitors contact-specific updates. The system identifies key events (promotions, job changes, funding rounds) and alerts the user with a pre-drafted, context-rich message.

The Impact

  • Engagement: Users reported a 3x increase in monthly professional interactions.
  • Automation: Reduced the time spent on news monitoring and outreach drafting by 15 hours per week.
  • Personalization: Achieved a 90% "Authenticity Score" in user testing for AI-generated outreach.
