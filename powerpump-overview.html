Project Overview

Turning Sedentary Downtime into Social Health Capital

Electric Vehicle (EV) charging can take 30-60 minutes, often leading to sedentary downtime for drivers. PowerPump is a hardware-software bridge that incentivizes physical activity during this window. By synchronizing real-time data from fitness wearables with EV charging session data, users earn rewards and "PowerPoints" for every calorie burned while their vehicle charges.
