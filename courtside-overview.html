Project Overview

The Future of Amateur Sports Analytics

Volleyball is a high-speed sport characterized by rapid player rotations, frequent occlusions, and a small, fast-moving ball. While professional leagues use expensive multi-camera systems, amateur and collegiate teams lack accessible analytics. Court-Side AI bridges this gap using specialized Computer Vision to automate highlight extraction and performance tracking from a single HD camera source.

Strategic Rationale

Technical Decision Making

Choosing the right tech stack was critical to balance the high-concurrency needs of real-time sports tracking with the accuracy required for professional-grade analytics.

Component Technology Strategic Rationale
Vision Model YOLOv8 Industry-leading inference speed (60fps) required for tracking sub-second ball movements.
Logic ByteTrack + Re-ID Maintains identity persistence through high-occlusion net plays where traditional trackers fail.
Geometry OpenCV Homography Mathematically maps 2D pixel space to a 3D court model for precise velocity metrics.
