Project Overview

Solving the Participation Gap in Professional Networking

Maintaining professional relationships is often a manual, fragmented process. Traditional CRMs are designed for sales pipelines, not the nuanced "weak ties" that drive career growth. Network Cafe uses LLMs to autonomously monitor news and contact updates, generating authentic outreach triggers that keep networks active without the manual overhead.

Strategic Rationale

Why AI-First?

Generic outreach is ignored. To drive real value, the system had to prioritize context and timing over volume.
